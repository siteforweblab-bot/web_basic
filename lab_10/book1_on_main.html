Plot

Eccentric detective Sherlock Holmes and his friend Dr. John Watson solve complex crimes in London, often with the help of Scotland Yard Inspector Lestrade. The stories are generally told from Watson's point of view, documenting their cases. Key recurring elements include Holmes's genius-level deductive reasoning, his clashes with criminals like Professor Moriarty, and the evolving partnership between the two central characters.
